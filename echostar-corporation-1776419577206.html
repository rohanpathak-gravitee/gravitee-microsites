Who Governs That Layer?

You've joined an organisation where GenAI adoption is already real and accelerating. The question isn't whether AI agents will call your APIs — they already do.

01
FCC Regulatory Compliance
Satellite operations are heavily regulated by the FCC. Unauthorized API calls impacting satellite positioning can trigger investigations and fines.
Financial penalties, reputational damage, and service disruptions.
Every new initiative adds another ungoverned integration point to your estate.
02
Real-time Data Processing
Network monitoring and satellite telemetry require high-throughput, low-latency data processing. Poorly managed APIs can introduce bottlenecks and impact service quality.
Reduced network performance, customer dissatisfaction, and potential loss of revenue.
03
API Security
Exposing APIs without proper authentication and authorization mechanisms increases the risk of data breaches and unauthorized access to critical systems.
Compromised customer data, service outages, and legal liabilities.
04
Cross-Domain API Governance
The merger with DISH introduces complexity in managing APIs across different business units. Lack of centralized governance can lead to scope violations and security risks.
Increased operational costs, security vulnerabilities, and compliance issues.
